In "Stealing Fire," Steven Kotler explores the pursuit of altered states of consciousness to achieve peak performance. Kotler delves into how elite organizations like the Navy SEALs, Silicon Valley tech companies, and cutting-edge scientists are harnessing these states for innovation and success. The book examines the science behind flow states, psychedelics, and other techniques for unlocking human potential.

"Stealing Fire" captivated me with its bold and unconventional approach to peak performance. Kotler’s exploration of flow states and the use of psychedelics in professional settings was both fascinating and provocative. It challenged my preconceived notions about what it means to achieve and sustain high performance. The book’s insights into the methods used by elite performers to tap into their full potential have practical applications for anyone looking to push the boundaries of their capabilities. It’s an intriguing and eye-opening read that offers a fresh perspective on what it takes to excel in today’s fast-paced world.
