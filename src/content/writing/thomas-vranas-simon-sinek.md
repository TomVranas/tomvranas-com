Simon Sinek's "Start with Why" explores the importance of purpose in leadership and business. Sinek argues that successful leaders and organizations start with a clear sense of why they exist, which inspires and motivates others. Through compelling examples, including Apple, Southwest Airlines, and Martin Luther King Jr., Sinek illustrates how starting with why leads to greater innovation, loyalty, and success.

Reading "Start with Why" was a revelation. Sinek’s insights into the power of purpose resonated deeply with my own leadership journey. The idea that great leaders inspire action by starting with why has profound implications for how we run our businesses and live our lives. It encouraged me to articulate my own "why" more clearly and to ensure that it drives my decisions and actions. This book is a powerful reminder that when we connect with our deeper purpose, we can inspire and lead others more effectively. It’s an essential read for anyone looking to lead with authenticity and vision.
